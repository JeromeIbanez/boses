---
title: Personas
description: Access and manage individual AI personas
---

**Personas** are the individual AI consumers generated within a persona group. Each has a consistent identity, backstory, and cultural grounding that persists across simulations.

## Endpoints

| Method | Path | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| `GET` | `/api/v1/projects/{project_id}/persona-groups/{group_id}/personas` | List all personas in a group |
| `GET` | `/api/v1/projects/{project_id}/persona-groups/{group_id}/personas/{persona_id}` | Get a single persona |
| `DELETE` | `/api/v1/projects/{project_id}/persona-groups/{group_id}/personas` | Delete all personas in a group |
| `DELETE` | `/api/v1/projects/{project_id}/persona-groups/{group_id}/personas/{persona_id}` | Delete a specific persona |

## Personas and the Library

When a persona is consistently high-performing across simulations, it can be promoted to the **Library** — a reusable pool of cross-project personas. See [Library](/api-reference/library) for details.

<Note>
Deleting personas from a group does not affect simulation results already recorded — past responses are preserved.
</Note>
