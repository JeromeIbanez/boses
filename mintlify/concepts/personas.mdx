A **persona** is an AI-generated individual within a persona group. Each one has a consistent identity, backstory, purchasing habits, and cultural context — and responds to simulations in character.

---

## What makes Boses personas different

Most AI personas are generic archetypes ("a 28-year-old female office worker in Manila"). Boses personas are grounded in real, current consumer signals from:

- **Reddit** — top posts from country-specific subreddits (r/Philippines, r/indonesia, r/VietNam)
- **Shopee** — product reviews from actual shoppers in each market
- **Google Play Store** — app reviews for dominant consumer apps (GCash/PH, Gojek/ID, MoMo/VN)

These signals are distilled into a **cultural context snapshot** per market, which is injected into every persona's generation prompt. This means personas carry real attitudes about specific products, pricing, trust, and digital habits — not generic cultural platitudes.

---

## Persona fields

Each persona includes:
- Full name and age
- Occupation and income level
- Personality traits and psychographic notes
- Trusted and distrusted brands
- Digital habits and preferred platforms
- Price sensitivity signals
- A profile photo (AI-generated avatar)

---

## Generating personas

```bash
POST /api/v1/projects/{project_id}/persona-groups/{group_id}/generate
```

Generation runs as a background task (20–40 seconds for a group of 5). The persona group's `generation_status` transitions from `pending` → `generating` → `complete`.

---

## Cultural snapshots

The ethnography pipeline runs automatically before persona generation. It crawls public sources, extracts signals via LLM, and stores a quality-gated snapshot per market.

You can also manually trigger a refresh — useful before a demo or when you want to prime the snapshot for a specific industry:

```bash
POST /api/v1/internal/ethnography/refresh/PH?vertical=fintech
```

Supported markets: `ID`, `PH`, `VN`.

<Note>
Snapshots are shared across all projects in the same market. They refresh automatically when a new persona group is created for that market.
</Note>
